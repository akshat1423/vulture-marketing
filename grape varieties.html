Grape Varieties

The essence of VULTURES champagne lies in our carefully selected grapes, each contributing unique characteristics to our distinctive blends.

The Grapes of VULTURES

VULTURES champagne is crafted from the noble grape varieties of the Champagne region, each bringing its own personality to our creations. Our winemakers skillfully blend these varieties to achieve the perfect balance of freshness, complexity, and elegance that defines the VULTURES style.

Pinot Noir

The backbone of VULTURES champagnes, Pinot Noir provides structure, body, and aromatic complexity. Our vineyards in the Montagne de Reims produce Pinot Noir with remarkable depth and aging potential.

The VULTURES Terroir

Our grapes thrive in the unique chalky soils of Champagne, which provide excellent drainage and impart distinctive mineral characteristics to our wines. The cool climate ensures slow, even ripening, preserving acidity while developing complex flavors.

Each vineyard parcel is carefully tended by our growers, many of whom have worked with the VULTURES house for generations. This partnership ensures the highest quality fruit that truly expresses our terroir.
